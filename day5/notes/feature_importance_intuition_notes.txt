===============================
FEATURE IMPORTANCE INTUITION
===============================

1. What is Feature Importance?
--------------
Feature Importance tells us
how much each input feature
contributes to the model's prediction.

--------------------------------

2. Why Feature Importance Matters
--------------
Not every feature contributes
equally to a prediction.

Some features have:
- High importance
- Medium importance
- Low importance

--------------------------------

3. Example
--------------
Employee Salary Prediction

Features:
- Experience
- Age
- Education

Label:
- Salary

Possible importance:

Experience → High

Education → Medium

Age → Low

Note:
Importance always depends
on the business problem.

--------------------------------

4. Business Dependency
--------------
The same feature can have
different importance
in different businesses.

Example:

Software Company

Experience → High

Education → Medium

Age → Low

Government Recruitment

Age may become
a highly important feature
because of eligibility rules.

--------------------------------

5. Relevant Features
--------------
Relevant features are
the features that help
the model make better predictions.

Example:

Experience

Education

Skills

--------------------------------

6. Irrelevant Features
--------------
Irrelevant features do not
have a meaningful relationship
with the prediction.

Example:

Favorite Color

--------------------------------

7. Can Irrelevant Features Affect
Model Performance?
--------------
Yes.

Irrelevant features may:

- introduce noise
- confuse the learning process
- reduce prediction quality
- increase training time

--------------------------------

8. More Features ≠ Better Model
--------------
Adding more features
does NOT always improve
model performance.

Better features
are more important
than more features.

--------------------------------

9. Business Examples
--------------
Feature: City

May influence salary because
different cities have
different salary standards.

Therefore:

City is business dependent.

--------------------------------

Feature: Favorite Color

Normally has no logical
relationship with salary.

Therefore:

It is usually an
irrelevant feature.

--------------------------------

10. Theory Reinforcement
--------------
Q: What is Feature Importance?

A:
Feature Importance tells us
how much each feature
contributes to the model's prediction.

--------------------------------

Q: Do all features contribute equally?

A:
No.

Different features contribute
differently depending on
the business problem.

--------------------------------

Q: Why is choosing the right
features important?

A:
Because relevant features help
the model make better
and more accurate predictions.

--------------------------------

Q: Can irrelevant features
reduce model performance?

A:
Yes.

They may introduce noise
and confuse the model,
which can reduce
prediction quality.

--------------------------------

11. Practical Reinforcement
--------------
Q: Is City always
an irrelevant feature?

A:
No.

Its importance depends
on the business problem.

--------------------------------

Q: Is Favorite Color
useful for salary prediction?

A:
No.

It usually has no logical
relationship with salary.

--------------------------------

12. Important Learning Principle
--------------
Think like an ML Engineer.

Do not ask:

"How many features do I have?"

Instead ask:

"Which features actually help
the model learn meaningful patterns?"
